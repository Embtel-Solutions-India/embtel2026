Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information Embtel Solutions collects, how we use it, and the choices you have over your data.

Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Embtel Solutions ("Embtel," "we," "us," or "our") provides web development, digital marketing, business automation, AI integration, and analytics services to small and medium businesses. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you visit our website embtelsolutions.com, contact us, or engage our services.

By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website or services.

2. Information We Collect

a) Information you provide directly

  • Contact details — your name, email address, phone number, company name, and job title when you fill out our contact form, request a proposal, subscribe to our newsletter, or schedule a strategy call.
  • Project information — details about your business, goals, requirements, budget, and timeline that you share with us during discovery, scoping, or service delivery.
  • Communications — emails, WhatsApp messages, calls, and notes from meetings or chats you have with our team.
  • Payment details — billing name, address, GST number, and bank or payment-processor references when you become a client. We do not store full credit-card numbers; payments are processed by trusted third-party gateways.

b) Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, time spent, links clicked, referring URLs, and approximate location derived from your IP address.
  • Device data — browser type and version, operating system, screen size, language, and device identifiers.
  • Cookies & similar technologies — see Section 5 below.

c) Information from third parties

We may receive information about you from analytics providers, advertising platforms (such as Google Ads, Meta Ads, LinkedIn Ads), and lead-generation tools you use to contact us.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to inquiries, provide quotes, and schedule strategy calls.
  • Deliver, manage, and improve the services you've engaged us for.
  • Send invoices, accept payments, and maintain accounting records as required by law.
  • Send service-related updates, project milestones, and important notices.
  • Send marketing emails and newsletters (only with your consent — you can opt out at any time).
  • Analyse website performance, debug technical issues, and improve our user experience.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access to our systems.
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from authorities.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers & subcontractors — trusted partners who help us deliver our services (e.g., hosting providers, email senders, payment processors, analytics tools, cloud storage). They access only the data necessary to perform their role and are bound by confidentiality.
  • Professional advisors — accountants, auditors, and legal counsel where required.
  • Legal compliance — when disclosure is required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.
  • Business transfers — if Embtel Solutions is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before this happens.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, measure traffic, and improve performance. Categories include:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function (e.g., security, navigation).
  • Analytics cookies — used by tools like Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site.
  • Marketing cookies — used to measure ad effectiveness and (where applicable) show relevant ads on third-party sites such as Google, Meta, and LinkedIn.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may limit website features.

6. Data Retention

We keep your personal information only as long as is necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable laws (such as tax and accounting regulations). Project files and client data are typically retained for the duration of the engagement plus a reasonable period afterward; you may request earlier deletion by contacting us.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard technical and organisational safeguards to protect your information — including encrypted data transfer (HTTPS/TLS), restricted access, secure cloud infrastructure, and regular backups. However, no method of transmission or storage on the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you believe your information has been compromised through our services, please contact us immediately at info@embtelsolutions.com.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws (including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, the EU GDPR, and the California CCPA), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format.
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data-protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@embtelsolutions.com. We will respond within 30 days.
